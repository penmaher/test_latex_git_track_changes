\section{Introduction}

Writing a journal article in \latex has so many advantages over writing in an office product such as word or libre office. But there are a few things \latex is missing. These include personalised track changes, personalised to-do notes and merging documents. So we wrote \emph{WRITE -- with git} to improve the writing experience in \latex. \Penny{\rev{1}}{Example to do note.}
